# FlavorForge - Presentation Guide
**Quick Reference: Which Presentation to Use**

---

## 📊 **TWO PRESENTATION VERSIONS**

### **1. REGULAR REVIEWS: 12-15 Slides**
**File**: `REVIEW_PRESENTATION_12-15_SLIDES.md`  
**Use For**:
- ✅ Mid-term reviews
- ✅ Progress presentations
- ✅ Regular check-ins
- ✅ Quick demonstrations

**Duration**: ~20 minutes + Q&A

**Content**:
- Problem & Solution
- Technologies
- System Architecture
- Key Technical Steps (TF-IDF, KNN)
- UI Screenshots
- Model Performance
- Challenges & Future

---

### **2. FINAL REVIEW: 40-45 Slides**
**File**: `FINAL_PRESENTATION_40-45_SLIDES.md`  
**Use For**:
- ✅ Final project presentation
- ✅ Professor review
- ✅ Internship demo
- ✅ Complete project showcase

**Duration**: ~46 minutes + Q&A (total 55-60 min)

**Content**:
- Complete introduction (5 slides)
- Detailed technical background (7 slides)
- Step-by-step implementation (10 slides)
- Full UI/UX showcase (8 slides)
- Navigation pages (3 slides)
- Detailed performance analysis (5 slides)
- Challenges & solutions (3 slides)
- Future improvements (3 slides)
- Conclusion (1 slide)

---

## 📋 **QUICK COMPARISON**

| Aspect | Regular (12-15) | Final (40-45) |
|--------|-----------------|---------------|

| **Detail Level** | Overview | Comprehensive |
