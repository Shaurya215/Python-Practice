About Us

FlavorForge is an academic project that helps you discover recipes based on your preferences. Enter your preferred cuisine, diet, prep time, and ingredients to get personalized recipe suggestions.

How it works

FlavorForge uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to convert your inputs and each recipe’s features (cuisine, diet, prep time, ingredients) into numeric vectors. It then uses K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) to find recipes in the dataset that are most similar to your preferences.

Technologies

Built with Flask (web framework), scikit-learn (TF-IDF and KNN), and pandas (data handling). The project is designed for academic demonstration and learning.
